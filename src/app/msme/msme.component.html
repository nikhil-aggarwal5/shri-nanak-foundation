Why MSMEs Matter

MSMEs are not a small part of the economy—they are the foundation.

~30%
Contribution to India's GDP
~45%
Share of India's exports
✦
Generate large-scale employment across the country

Yet most MSMEs struggle with:

  • Lack of awareness about schemes
  • Difficulty accessing finance
  • Compliance challenges
  • Delayed payments and missed opportunities
What this really means is: benefits exist — but they don't reach the people who need them most.
🎯

Our Vision

We are committed to empowering India's entrepreneurial spirit by bridging the gap between government schemes and grassroots-level businesses. At Shri Nanak Foundation, we believe that informed entrepreneurs are the foundation of a Viksit Bharat 2047. Through dedicated support, awareness programs, and on-ground assistance, we help small business owners, aspiring entrepreneurs, women entrepreneurs to unlock the full potential of MSME-related benefits and policies.

🚀

Our Mission

  • Increase MSME awareness at the ground level
  • Enable access to government schemes and finance
  • Support compliance and formalization
  • Build long-term relationships with entrepreneurs
